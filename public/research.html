My PhD thesis is about existence concepts and negative existentials in the works of Frege, Moore, Russell, and the early Wittgenstein. Among other things, I argue that Frege's account of singular existentials has generally been misunderstood and develop a novel interpretation of Russell's theory of denoting concepts in Principles of Mathematics.

I have also worked on ontological pluralism, the ontology of fictional characters, the ontology and epistemology of musical works, expressive limitations of two-dimensional modal logic and neo-Carnapian metaontology, among various other topics in the philosophy of language, metaphysics and logic.
