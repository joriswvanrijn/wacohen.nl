I am a PhD student in philosophy at Gonville and Caius College, University of Cambridge.

My PhD research is mainly in (meta)metaphysics, history of analytic philosophy and philosophy of language, but I am also interested in logic and philosophy of music.

I have also written some short essays in Dutch aimed at a non-academic audience. Some of these are about topics related to my research, some are rather on issues in social philosophy.

You can contact me at wac36[at]cam.ac.uk.
