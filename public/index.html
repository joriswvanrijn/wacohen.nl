I am a post-doc in philosophy at the University of Manchester, funded by an Analysis Studentship. I completed my PhD at the University of Cambridge (Gonville and Caius College) in 2023.

My research is mainly in the history of analytic philosophy and (meta)metaphysics, but I am also interested in logic, philosophy of language and philosophy of music.

You can contact me at wouter.cohen@manchester.ac.uk.

My PhilPeople profile is here.

I also write popular philosophy (in Dutch) at BijNaderInzien, where I am a regular columnist.
