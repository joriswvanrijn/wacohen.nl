I am a PhD student in philosophy at Gonville and Caius College, University of Cambridge.

My PhD research is mainly in (meta)metaphysics, history of analytic philosophy and philosophy of language, but I am also interested in philosophy of music and logic.

I have written some short essays on topics in social and political philosophy of language. These are in Dutch and aimed at a non-academic audience.

You can contact me at wac36[at]cam.ac.uk.
